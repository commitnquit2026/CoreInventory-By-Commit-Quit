CoreInventory – Unique Selling Points (Virtual Round)
Team: Commit&Quit

------------------------------------------------
1. Real-World Inventory Workflow
------------------------------------------------
The system models real business operations including
receipts, deliveries, internal transfers, and stock adjustments.

------------------------------------------------
2. Real-Time Stock Updates
------------------------------------------------
Inventory levels automatically update whenever goods
are received, delivered, or transferred.

------------------------------------------------
3. Local Database System
------------------------------------------------
The application runs entirely on a local database,
ensuring fast performance and minimal dependency
on cloud services.

------------------------------------------------
4. Minimal Third-Party Dependency
------------------------------------------------
The system is built with very few external libraries,
making it reliable and easy to deploy in production.

------------------------------------------------
5. Complete Stock Movement History
------------------------------------------------
Every inventory action is logged in a stock ledger
to provide full traceability.

------------------------------------------------
6. Multi-Warehouse Support
------------------------------------------------
Businesses can manage inventory across multiple
warehouses and locations.

------------------------------------------------
7. Smart Product Search
------------------------------------------------
Products can be quickly located using SKU search
and filtering by category or warehouse.

------------------------------------------------
8. Low Stock Alerts
------------------------------------------------
The system notifies managers when stock reaches
a critical level.

------------------------------------------------
9. Modular Architecture
------------------------------------------------
The system is built using modular components,
making it easy to expand and maintain.

------------------------------------------------
10. Clean and Interactive UI
------------------------------------------------
The interface is designed to be simple,
efficient, and easy for warehouse staff to use.
